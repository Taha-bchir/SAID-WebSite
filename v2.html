About Us

Our Story

SAID was born in 1947, in a small kitchen in the bustling city of Paris. Marie Dubois, a passionate young woman with a love for all things sweet, began experimenting with chocolate recipes, blending traditional techniques with her own unique flair. Her creations, rich in flavor and crafted with the finest ingredients, quickly gained popularity amongst friends and family.

Word of Marie's talent spread, and soon she was receiving requests for her chocolates from local shops and cafes. In 1952, with the help of her husband Jean, Marie opened her first chocolate shop, a charming space filled with the tantalizing aroma of freshly roasted cacao beans and handcrafted treats. The shop quickly became a beloved destination for Parisians, drawn to the unique flavors and exquisite quality of Chocolaty Delights' confections.

Over the next few decades, Chocolaty Delights expanded steadily. Marie and Jean, always dedicated to quality and innovation, introduced new flavors and products, from delicate truffles and pralines to decadent chocolate bars and festive seasonal collections. Their commitment to ethical sourcing and sustainable practices resonated with customers, further solidifying their reputation as a responsible and socially conscious company.

The 1980s saw Chocolaty Delights venturing beyond Paris, opening its first international store in London. This marked the beginning of a global expansion that would see the company establish a presence in major cities across Europe, Asia, and North America. Despite its growing size and international reach, Chocolaty Delights remained true to its core values, always prioritizing quality ingredients, traditional craftsmanship, and innovative flavors.

Today, Chocolaty Delights is a world-renowned brand, synonymous with luxurious chocolates and exceptional quality. The company continues to innovate, introducing new collections and collaborating with renowned chefs and artists. Yet, it remains true to its humble beginnings, honoring the legacy of Marie Dubois and her passion for creating joy through chocolate.

Our Mission

At Our Company, our mission is to provide high-quality products/services that meet the needs of our customers. We strive for excellence in everything we do, from product development to customer service.
